SLIDE 2 – PROBLEM DEFINITION
─────────────────────────────
Selected Domain: Healthcare and Life Sciences

Problem Statement:
• Chronic Kidney Disease affects 850 MILLION people worldwide
• Nearly 90% of CKD cases are detected ONLY in late stages
• By the time symptoms appear, kidneys are already 70-85% damaged
• India has the 2nd highest CKD burden globally

Why This Matters:
• Late detection leads to dialysis (₹15,000-25,000/month) or transplant (₹5-15 lakhs)
• Early detection at Stage 1-2 can PREVENT progression
• No accessible AI-based screening tool exists for early detection

Project Title: RenalGuard AI


SLIDE 3 – EXISTING SOLUTIONS AND GAP
─────────────────────────────────────
Existing Solutions:
• eGFR calculators in hospitals
• Manual diagnosis by nephrologists
• Expensive diagnostic equipment
• Hospital-based clinical software

Gaps Identified:
❌ No AI-based screening tools
❌ Limited accessibility in rural healthcare
❌ No explainable AI for medical predictions
❌ No automated clinical reports
❌ No decision-support for healthcare workers

Statistics:
• 80% of CKD deaths occur in developing countries
• Rural populations lack access to specialists
• Average delay in diagnosis: 3-5 years

SLIDE 5 – PROOF OF CONCEPT FOR MVP
───────────────────────────────────
Working Prototype Flow:

Step 1: User enters patient test parameters
        → Simple form interface
        → 24 clinical parameters

Step 2: Machine learning model predicts CKD risk
        → Binary classification: CKD/Not CKD
        → Risk score: 0-100

Step 3: System estimates CKD stage
        → Stage 1-5 classification
        → eGFR calculation

Step 4: SHAP explainability shows contributing factors
        → Waterfall plot visualization
        → Feature contribution percentages

Step 5: AI assistant explains results
        → Natural language explanation
        → Q&A capability

Step 6: Clinical PDF report generated
        → Downloadable report
        → Patient-friendly format
