================================================================================
                        RECORDING TIPS
================================================================================

BEFORE RECORDING:
1. Practice the demo 2-3 times
2. Ensure all pages load quickly
3. Have sample data ready
4. Close unnecessary applications
5. Test your microphone

DURING RECORDING:
1. Speak clearly and at moderate pace
2. Move mouse slowly and deliberately
3. Pause between sections
4. Don't rush through explanations

AFTER RECORDING:
1. Review the video
2. Edit if needed (remove long pauses)
3. Add captions if possible
4. Export in 1080p
5. Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Google Drive

RECOMMENDED TOOLS:
• OBS Studio (Free, feature-rich)
• Loom (Free tier available, easy to use)
• Camtasia (Paid, professional)
• Windows Game Bar (Built-in Windows 10/11)
• QuickTime (Built-in macOS)
